We adjust the ratio of Indexer‘s totalStakedAmount and sumDailyRewards by setting the value of threshold. A smaller threshold value means that the Indexer can get higher sumDailyRewards with a smaller totalStakedAmount，vice versa. If the threshold is less than PER_MILL, we will not limit the indexer's sumDailyRewards.
